# Marketing-Campaign-Performance-Analysis

## 1. Project Title

Marketing Campaign Performance Analysis Dashboard

---

## 2. Short Description / Purpose

This project analyzes marketing campaign performance across multiple regions and industries to identify high-performing campaigns, evaluate return on investment (ROI), optimize marketing spend, and support data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard provides insights into campaign effectiveness, revenue generation, customer engagement, and regional performance through interactive visualizations.

---

## 3. Tech Stack

### Tools Used

* Power BI – Dashboard Development & Visualization
* SQL – Data Cleaning, Transformation & Analysis
* Excel – Data Preparation and Validation

---

## 4. Data Source

The dataset consists of three related tables:

### Campaign Performance

Contains campaign-level metrics including:

* Campaign ID
* Campaign Name
* Region
* Industry
* Spend
* Revenue
* ROI
* Impressions
* Clicks
* Conversions

### Campaign Details

Contains campaign classification information:

* Campaign Name
* Campaign Type
* Average Spend
* Average ROI

### Region Performance

Contains regional KPIs:

* Region
* Total Revenue
* Total Spend
* Average ROI

Relationships were created using:

* Campaign Name
* Region

---

## 5. Features / Highlights

### Business Problem

Marketing teams invest significant budgets into campaigns but often lack visibility into:

* Which campaigns drive the highest revenue
* Which campaign types generate the best ROI
* Which regions perform most effectively
* How engagement metrics affect conversions
* Where marketing budgets can be optimized

Without proper analysis, organizations risk overspending on underperforming campaigns and missing opportunities for growth.

---

### Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard was designed to:

* Track overall marketing performance
* Monitor campaign ROI
* Compare regional performance
* Analyze customer engagement metrics
* Identify optimization opportunities
* Support strategic marketing decisions

---

### Walkthrough of Key Visuals

#### KPI Cards

Provide a quick overview of:

* Total Revenue
* Total Spend
* Average ROI
* Total Conversions

#### Revenue by Campaign

Highlights top-performing campaigns based on revenue generation.

#### ROI Analysis

Compares campaign profitability and identifies the most efficient campaigns.

#### Revenue by Region

Shows geographical performance and market opportunities.

#### Campaign Type Performance

Evaluates the effectiveness of different campaign categories.

#### Conversion Analysis

Measures campaign success in converting customer engagement into business outcomes.

---

### Business Impact & Insights

#### Key Insights

* A small number of campaigns contribute the majority of revenue.
* Certain campaign types consistently achieve higher ROI.
* Some regions outperform others despite lower marketing spend.
* High-spend campaigns do not always generate the highest returns.
* Strong conversion rates are closely linked to campaign profitability.

#### Business Impact

* Improved marketing budget allocation.
* Better identification of profitable campaigns.
* Increased focus on high-performing regions.
* Enhanced decision-making through real-time performance monitoring.
* Greater efficiency in future marketing investments.
